Restaurant Beta Users

We've been working on the use of checklists in restaurants with The Roman Candle. TRC has been using Manifestly on their admin checklists and they're very happy thus far.

There are many processes within restaurants that are golden opportunities for checklists, Daily Store Opening, Closing, Weekly reviews can all be better performed with a checklist software system like Manifestly. We're excited to be working with TRC as they grow to more stores in Wisconsin and we congratulate them on their new restaurant in Milwaukee.

For any other restaurants, for a limited time we're offering our checklist service for free. Forever. Just contact us at support@manifest.ly to get started on using software to manage your restaurant checklists.
